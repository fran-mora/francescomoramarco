Map of Lothinias

A continent the size of Australia, but with dragons. This is the story world of The Trickster, At all costs, and The Contract.

The Trickster
 
Work in progress

Aged 13, all Nero wants is an apprenticeship and to win the attention of ELVA, a street illusionist. When Elva gets into trouble for tricking a member of the local mafia — the Hades — Nero helps her out and asks her to show him the illusion.

Everything changes when his mother falls very ill, and he has to find the means to buy her expensive medicine. Mentored by Elva’s father, Nero sets up a street performance that earns him both the money for the medicine and Elva’s long-sought attention.

But his rising popularity brings him to the attention of the Hades, who will do anything in their power to crush him.

The Contract
 

Assassination is a thing of the past. Her joints ache, her breath is short, and her heart can't bear the toll of another death. Yet she's signed a contract, her final contract. And it's the hardest she's ever had to take, and not just because if she fails her entire people will be wiped out.

The Contract is a silver honourable mention of Writers of the Future, 2020.

Six strangers and an elephant
 

There once was a lone dirt road. It wound its way through thick jungles, shouldered sharp cliffs, and crossed villages too small to get names of their own. On this very day, a child in a brown dress happened to walk down the road, so that it was no longer lonely. But the child was. Her head bobbed up and down in weariness. Her feet hurt on the small chalk pebbles underneath as she had left her sandals miles behind. Her long dark hair swept along the waist of her dress, which was singed along the edges. She was hungry, tired, and thirsty, but she wasn't lost. She was heading for Keluarga and knew that the lone road would take her there, eventually. ...

Six strangers and an elephant is a silver honourable mention of Writers of the Future, 2022.
